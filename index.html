Our Story

A Neighborhood Bakery
Built on Trust & Taste

Kushal Iyengar's Cake Mane is a trusted neighborhood bakery in Bashettihalli, Bengaluru, serving freshly baked cakes, snacks, and authentic Iyengar bakery specialties. Known for quality, affordability, and delicious taste, we bring smiles to every celebration and warmth to every morning.

Every item on our shelves is baked fresh daily using quality ingredients. Whether it's a birthday cake, a box of puffs for the office, or a sweet khara bun with morning chai — we're here for every moment that matters.
